AI Apocalypse

Main Description

A 2D top-down fun shooter game to play. The goal is to get the maximum kills. It's free, with no ads and no in-app purchases. There might be some bugs, but it's not the main game I am working on. However, please download it to support us! You can shoot lasers and grenades. The enemies are AI like ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini, and more. This game is also available on Android in the Play Store, but there won't be many updates. I hope you download it! Available for Android, Windows, and Linux on itch.io and Play Store.

More Explained Description

The game is a shooter top-down 2D game. You can shoot grenades by right-clicking and lasers by left-clicking. Your goal is to get maximum kills and beat your high score. If you run out of ammo or grenades, you can shoot crates around to receive some ammo. Get ready for an amazing experience!
